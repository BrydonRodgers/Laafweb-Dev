Floor Screeding

A proper screed is the foundation of every quality floor finish. We lay smooth, level floor screeds that provide the perfect base for tiles, epoxy coatings, vinyl, or polished concrete — ensuring your final floor surface looks great and lasts for years.

We handle screeding for new builds, renovations, and commercial floor preparations across the West Coast, including Vredenburg and Saldanha.

  • Sand and cement floor screeding
  • Level and smooth screed for tile installation
  • Screed for epoxy and resin flooring
  • Underfloor heating screed systems
  • Garage and workshop floors
  • Commercial and industrial screeding
  • Patching and repair screeds

Decking

A quality deck transforms your outdoor space — perfect for the West Coast lifestyle. We design and build timber and composite decks for entertainment areas, braai patios, poolsides, and seaside properties that can handle the coastal environment.

  • Braai and entertainment deck areas

  • Deck repairs and restoration

Painting

The final coat makes or breaks a build. Our painting team delivers clean, consistent finishes on interior walls and ceilings, and durable exterior coats that stand up to the West Coast's harsh sun, wind, and salt air.

We surface-prepare properly — fixing cracks, sealing, and priming before any paint goes on — because a lasting finish starts well before the brush touches the wall.

  • Interior walls and ceilings — new builds and renovations
  • Exterior painting and weatherproofing coats
  • Plaster primer and first / second coat application
  • Feature walls and specialty finishes
  • Garage floors and industrial coatings
  • Crack and defect repair before painting
  • Fascias, gutters and outbuilding painting

Waterproofing

Water ingress is the number one cause of structural damage in the Western Cape. On the West Coast, where salty air and seasonal rain are relentless, proper waterproofing isn't optional — it's essential.

We waterproof roofs, wet areas, basements, retaining walls and balconies using proven systems that hold up to West Coast weather conditions. If you're seeing damp, leaks, or rising moisture — call us before it gets worse.

  • Flat roof and pitched roof waterproofing
  • Bathroom and shower waterproofing (pre-tile)
  • Balcony and terrace waterproofing
  • External wall damp treatment
  • Basement and below-slab waterproofing
  • Crystalline and membrane waterproofing systems
  • Leak detection and remedial waterproofing
